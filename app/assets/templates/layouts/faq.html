Frequently Asked Questions about Angel Care and CDPAP
Most popular questions
Am I eligible for these program?
If you:

  • Have Medicaid

  • Live in New York

  • Need assistance with activities of daily living?

you may be eligible for services.
Who can I hire as a caregiver?

If you are interested in CDPAP and want to know who can become a caregiver here are some options:

  • Friend
  • Son or Daughter In Laws
  • Siblings
  • Children
Where is Angel Care available?

Proudly serving all 5 boroughs of New York City and Nassau County

General questions
How much do you pay?

You will need to have your HHA certificate, a recent physical (within the last 12 months) with all lab reports including Rubella/Measles, PPD test or Quantiferon Gold Assay (if positive a chest x-ray is required), a drug test, 2 forms of ID (employment auth, permanent resident card, passport, social security card, student ID, state or federal ID) and 2 typed reference letters. You can call our office and we will schedule a day for you to come in and apply. If you have all of your paperwork, you may be able to attend your orientation right away and get working!
